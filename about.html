Meet the team

Our Stem Racing team has 6 members with different roles, responsibilities and skills, and everyone does their job.

Adriana Gabriel

Project Engeneer

She designs the car and the stand. Tests and optimizes the car using both CAD and physical prototypes, ensuring everything is within the rules and regulations. Being also responsible for creating our technical drawings and website.

João Branco

Resouces Manager

He secured our sponsorships, building partnerships and managing budgets. He contacts them and discusses our proposal in meetings, both online and in person. Also organizes our agenda and calendar.

Alexandra Buyan

Social Media Manager

She is responsible for our social media, she took photos and recorded part of our process and posted them on our social media, making sure we had contact with the public.

Matilde Gonçalves

Marketing

Helps our marketing director with our social media, making more posts than recording or taking photos. Builds public presence and interacts online.

Diogo Alves

Pilot

Launches the car during races, has a good reaction time and represents technical performance in events. He is the 'face' of our team.

Bruna Pontes

Creative Designer

She makes sure we have an identity. She designs our logo, branding, merchandising, visual assets and the portfolio design.
